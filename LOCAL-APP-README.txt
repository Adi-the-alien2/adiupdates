AdiUpdates - Local App
======================

How to run:
1) Make sure Node.js is installed (https://nodejs.org).
2) Double-click: Run-AdiUpdates.bat
3) Your browser will open at http://localhost:3000

To stop the app:
- Close the terminal window that opened.

Share this app:
- You can send the whole folder or the zip file:
  AdiUpdates-local.zip
